About Lebo

Lebo started his career performing stand-up at local markets before achieving viral fame in 2023 with his signature "Eswatini Traffic Light" skit. Known for his clean, family-friendly humor and incredibly clever wordplay, he has become a household name, bridging the gap between traditional comedy and modern social media entertainment.

He actively promotes local talent and is often featured in Street Talk's comedy spotlight segment. His goal is to take Eswatini comedy to the global stage while maintaining strong roots in local culture and current affairs.
